Akan Names

This is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names. Luckily for us, there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date. Well,no need to worry because the solution is here.
